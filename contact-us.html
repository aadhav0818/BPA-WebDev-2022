FAQ

What are your hours of operation?
They are open from Monday through Friday from 7:30 AM - 7:00 PM and Saturday through Sunday from 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM.
How do you determine the price of your used vehicles?
We use a variety of factors to determine the price of our used vehicles, including the make, model, age, condition, and mileage of the vehicle, as well as market demand and comparables. We strive to offer fair and competitive prices on all of our used vehicles, and we encourage you to shop around and compare prices before making a decision.
Do your used vehicles come with a warranty?
Many of our used vehicles come with a limited warranty, which can provide some protection against unexpected repairs. The specific terms of the warranty will vary depending on the age and condition of the vehicle, as well as the manufacturer's original warranty. We encourage you to ask about the available warranty options when shopping for a used vehicle.
Do you offer any services for maintaining and repairing used vehicles?
Yes, we offer a range of services for maintaining and repairing used vehicles, including oil changes, tire rotations, brake repairs, and more. We have a team of experienced technicians who are equipped to handle all types of repairs, and we use only high-quality parts and equipment. In addition, we offer a variety of service specials and plans to help you save money on your maintenance and repair needs.
Do you offer financing for used vehicles?
Yes, we offer financing options for our used vehicles. Our financing team can work with you to find a loan or leasing option that fits your budget and financial needs. We have relationships with a variety of lenders, so we can help you secure a competitive rate and flexible terms.
